Brief History

The school is located in giwa LGA of kaduna Nigeria , it was founded by Imam Suleiman Madawaki Giwa in 2009, to which he donated it to the community after the establishmet.

The School Runs Various Sections Apart from the western education, the school runs Islamic School in the afternoon including Arabic Classes to which married women are being taught as well as those who need agency for mass education.

Our school is also renowned for its vibrant community spirit that welcomes diversity and fosters a supportive environment. We believe in creating strong bonds within our community, facilitating an engaging and secure atmosphere where students are motivated to achieve their fullest potential. Excellent Community School, Giwa, not only prepares students for academic success but also instills essential life skills, making them resilient, respectful, and responsible citizens of the world.

Our Mission

To mould Science, Art nd Religious students, through employying enough qualified teachers and providing enough facilities and equipments to help them gain vast knowledge and to enlarge their view on various careers for self employments.

Our Visson

To provide enable environment with a standards, that will provide all the necessary educational facilities,that will impact adequately to students and pupils of the community with different talents in order to make them to be Useful to themselves, to the community and the Nation in general

Frequently Asked Questions - Excellent Community School Giwa

School hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

We offer a range of activities including sports, music, arts, and various clubs and societies.

Our school provides individualized support plans, dedicated staff, and specialized resources to assist students with special educational needs.

Admission requirements include a completed application form, previous school records, and a successful entrance examination.

We encourage parent involvement through the PTA, volunteer opportunities, and regular parent-teacher meetings.

Our school ensures safety with on-site security personnel, CCTV surveillance, and strict visitor screening procedures.

We have a comprehensive emergency response plan, regular drills, and staff trained in first aid and emergency response.
